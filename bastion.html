Chapter I

🏰 The Fortress

◇ ◆ ◇

Descend into the deep twilight of Blackreach and set foot in a stronghold risen from ruin and memory. Silk banners drape the ancient walls, scented braziers burn with undying flame — a warmth that gives no comfort, carrying with it the faint, lingering trace of incense long since forgotten. Gargoyle sentinels loom over bridge and bastion alike, as still and silent as the stone that birthed them.

Most visitors don’t notice anything special at first. They step inside, marvel at the stonework, the banners, the decorum. Then it settles in — that sensation between the shoulder blades. The unmistakable feeling of being observed. Appraised.

The grand entrance — silk banners, undying flame
Gargoyle sentinels watch in silence
Every corner, appraised

Chapter II

♛ The Crimson Hall

◇ ◆ ◇

Walk the Crimson Hall, where Sanguine’s favour lives in the very air you breathe! Bloodwine flows without end, music bleeds into warm crimson-lit stone, and laughter drifts through halls whose walls have witnessed more than any living soul could tell. The light is low. Crimson. Forgiving. Every goblet raised is a tribute to the Lord of Revelry, every dance a step in his eternal feast.

You don’t enter this feast. You are absorbed into it.
Whether you mean to or not.
The feast hall — the feast never ends
Where the records are kept
Lord of Revelry — presiding over the hall
The bloodwine barrel
A quiet corner — if you can find one
Great bloodwine barrel - never runs dry

Chapter III

📖 The Scarlet Archive

◇ ◆ ◇

Not all temptations come wrapped in silk.

Beyond the revelry lies the Archive — modest in size, yet holding more forbidden knowledge than the grand libraries of Tamriel’s greatest mages. Dust hangs in the air like a veil drawn between ages, and every shelf here is worth more than most castles. Ancient tomes and scrolls so brittle their parchment survives only by enchantments that shimmer faintly in the candlelight.

Ayleid texts, Daedric treatises, histories of bloodlines erased by fire, war, or deliberate forgetting — the kind of secrets scholars would trade years of life for, sitting here quietly gathering dust. Trace your fingers along the spines — warmth in one, a cold prickle in the next. Some books hum softly when no one is looking. Not everything here is bound in paper. Not everything wants to be read.

The Archive
Not everything wants to be read
A scholar’s alcove

Chapter IV

🔥 The Hidden Path

▪ ◆ ▪

And for those bold enough to linger — a chimney crackles in the library, same as any other. Warm. Ordinary. Inviting, even. But stay a while and you’ll notice — smoke should drift. This smoke climbs. Slow. Purposeful. Coiling upward as if tracing a path already known.

They say this chimney hides a passage — a secret path along a forgotten road carved through the rock to an ancient Ayleid gate. And beyond it? The Temple of Molag Bal. A place where even revelry knows to keep its voice down.

But that’s only a rumor.
And you didn’t hear it here.
The hidden road
Stone steps toward something older
The gate — where even revelry keeps its voice down
