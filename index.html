Abagarlas

Notes on the Passage Beyond
◇ Through the Secret Passage, Beyond a Forgotten Gate ◇

Ayleid ruin above the Bastion, reached through a chimney passage in the library and a forgotten road cut through stone that predates the clan by ages. Black soul gems pulse in the corridors like something breathing in its sleep. There’s a throne at the top that was built for worship — empty when we found it, still humming. The veil into Coldharbour is open. Not torn. Not fraying. Open. And at the very end, a forge running on Aetherial light in a place where no such light should be possible.

The Craft Behind the Stone

◇ — — ◆ — — ◇

Environmental Storytelling

A room should tell you something before you read a single note. The way light falls, what’s left on a table, how a corridor narrows the deeper you go. If the space doesn’t speak on its own, it’s not done yet.

Structural Intent

No wall is decorative. No furnishing is filler. If a room exists, it exists for a reason — and if that reason isn’t clear, the room gets torn down and rebuilt until it is.

Long-Form Builder

These aren’t weekend projects. Some builds take months. The lore grows alongside the structure, and sometimes the story changes the floor plan halfway through. That’s part of the process.
